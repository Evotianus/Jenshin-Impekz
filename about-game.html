Do Daily Commissions

Obviously, the main thing Genshin Impact players need to do daily is the Daily Commissions. Each day, four different tasks will be available, each granting 10 Primogems upon completion. Then, if all four quests are finished, Travelers can speak to Katheryne to earn an extra 20 Primogems.

Farm Ores And Go To The Blacksmith

First, players should go to the Blacksmith and ask for Ore Vein's location. These Veins often contain more Crystal Chunk, so for those with limited play time, this can be their minimum effort.

Farm Random Events

Random Event in Genshin Impact is something that occurs in the open world as players explore Teyvat. However, while it's called a “Random Event”, it's actually not random at all. People can actually get these quests on purpose by visiting its trigger area.

Finish Battle Pass Tasks

Battle Pass grants a decent amount of rewards even if players choose to not unlock its premium version. However, to complete it, people need to slowly farm its EXP. Every week, the Battle Pass is capped at 10,000 EXP (10 levels), so it needs five weeks to complete.
